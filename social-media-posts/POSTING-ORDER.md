## Notes

- Always put the product name (Octively / octively.com) in replies and comments before you do in posts.
  People who engage will see it. Lower friction than a cold announcement.
- After Week 4, you can use the product name freely in posts because the audience has context.
- Before posting any of these, run through `/humanizer-main` one more time to check for em dashes or AI phrases.
- Add the post date to the filename when you actually post (e.g. rename to `2026-06-09-founder-story...` for the actual send date).
