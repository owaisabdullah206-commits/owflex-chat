The most common question I get from freelancers: "How do I bring up an AI chatbot to a client?"

Here's the exact framing that works.

Not: "I can build you an AI chatbot."
That sounds like you're selling something.

Instead, start with what they already told you:

"You mentioned you get a lot of calls about [appointments/pricing/products]. I've been setting up something that handles those automatically. Would it help if I showed you a quick demo on your site?"

That's it.

You're not pitching.
You're connecting something they already said to something you can already do.

What happens next:
👉 They say yes, you show the demo live
👉 They ask questions, you answer honestly
👉 They ask the price, you say ₨10,000/month, includes setup and monthly check-ins

If they say no, that's fine.
You learned something about that client.
Move to the next one.

The freelancers making ₨30,000 to ₨50,000/month from AI retainers are not technical wizards.
They are comfortable having this conversation.

The tool is the easy part.
The conversation is the skill.
