Most "free" AI chatbots give you 20 messages a month, then ask for a card.

I checked the popular ones before building mine.

Chatbase: 50 messages a month.
Tidio: 50, and the AI is a paid add-on.
Denser: 20.
Most of the rest: a 7-day trial, then a bill.

For a small business site, 20 to 50 messages is gone in the first week.

So the free plan on what I am building works differently:
👉 1 bot
👉 200 conversations a month
👉 A real knowledge base, so it answers from your own content
👉 No credit card

The point is not to give everything away.

It is to let a freelancer test a real bot on a real client site before paying anything. You only move to a paid plan when you need more bots or want to drop the branding.
