# Noor Dental Clinic - Patient Guide

Noor Dental is a general and cosmetic dentistry clinic in Lahore, serving patients
since 2018. This guide covers our services, fees, appointment process, and policies
so you can assist patients quickly and accurately.

## About Us

Noor Dental is a family-run clinic in DHA Phase 5, Lahore. We offer general dentistry,
orthodontics, cosmetic treatments, and emergency dental care. All procedures are
performed by BDS-qualified dentists with post-graduate training.

## Clinic Timings

- Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
- Sunday: 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM (emergencies and pre-booked appointments only).
- Public holidays: Closed except for dental emergencies — call ahead.

## Location

- **Lahore - DHA Phase 5:** Clinic 7, Sehar Commercial, Lane 5.
- Free parking is available outside the building.
- We do not have a branch in Karachi or Islamabad at this time.

## Services and Fees

All prices are in Pakistani Rupees (PKR) and include consultation.

### General Dentistry
- Consultation and checkup — Rs. 500
- Dental cleaning (scaling) — Rs. 1,800
- Tooth filling (composite) — Rs. 2,500 per tooth
- Simple tooth extraction — Rs. 1,500
- Surgical extraction / wisdom tooth — Rs. 4,000–6,000

### Cosmetic Dentistry
- Teeth whitening (in-clinic, both arches) — Rs. 8,000
- Dental veneer (per tooth) — Rs. 12,000
- Smile makeover package (consultation required) — customised quote

### Orthodontics
- Metal braces (full treatment, both arches) — Rs. 55,000–65,000
- Clear aligners (full treatment, both arches) — Rs. 90,000–110,000
- Retainers (after braces) — Rs. 4,000 per arch

### Pediatric Dentistry
- Children under 12 get a 15% discount on all general dentistry procedures.

## Appointment Booking

- Appointments can be booked by WhatsApp, phone call, or walk-in.
- We recommend booking at least 2 days in advance for non-emergency visits.
- For orthodontic consultations, bring any previous X-rays if available.
- Walk-ins are welcome for checkups but may need to wait 30–60 minutes.

## X-Rays

- Digital dental X-rays are done in-clinic on the same day.
- Full mouth X-ray (OPG) — Rs. 1,200.
- Periapical X-ray (single tooth) — Rs. 400.

## Payment Methods

- Cash is accepted at reception.
- Easypaisa and JazzCash transfers are accepted.
- Bank transfer is available — ask the receptionist for account details.
- We do not accept credit or debit cards at this time.

## Cancellations and No-Shows

- Please cancel or reschedule at least 4 hours before your appointment.
- Repeated no-shows may require a Rs. 500 advance deposit for future bookings.

## Emergency Dental Care

- Toothache, lost filling, chipped tooth, or swelling — call us directly.
- We reserve slots daily for same-day emergency cases.
- Emergency consultation fee: Rs. 800.
