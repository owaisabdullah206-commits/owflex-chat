# Auraline Cosmetics - Store Guide

Auraline Cosmetics is a Pakistani beauty brand offering skincare and makeup made for local
skin tones and climate. This guide covers our products, prices, delivery, payments, and
returns so you can help customers quickly and accurately.

## About Us

Auraline started in Lahore in 2021 with a simple goal: clean, affordable beauty that actually
suits Pakistani skin. Every product is dermatologically tested and free from parabens and
sulfates. We ship nationwide and have two physical stores in Lahore.

## Store Timings

- Open every day, 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM (including weekends).
- Closed only on the first day of Eid.
- Online orders can be placed 24/7 on our website.

## Store Locations

- **Lahore - Johar Town:** Shop 14, Maratib Ali Road, Block G1.
- **Lahore - DHA:** Y Block Commercial, Phase 3.

## Delivery and Shipping

- We deliver across Pakistan, including Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Multan, and smaller cities.
- Standard delivery takes 2 to 3 working days. Remote areas may take up to 5 days.
- Delivery in Lahore (including Johar Town and DHA) is often next-day.
- Delivery charges: Rs. 200 flat, free on orders above Rs. 3,000.
- We do not ship internationally at this time.

## Payment Methods

- Cash on Delivery (COD) is available nationwide.
- We accept Easypaisa and JazzCash.
- Bank transfer and debit or credit cards are accepted on the website.

## Returns and Exchange

- Items can be exchanged within 7 days of delivery, with the receipt and original packaging.
- Opened or used products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons, unless the item is faulty.
- Sale items are final and not eligible for exchange.
- To start an exchange, the customer should contact our team with their order number.
